Honey Badger Gift Notification Service

Last Updated: February 18, 2026

About Honey Badger

Honey Badger is a gift notification platform that connects gift senders with recipients through SMS and email notifications. Our service allows users to send gifts with motivational challenges, creating meaningful and engaging gifting experiences.

How We Collect SMS Consent

Consent for SMS messages from Honey Badger is collected in the following ways:

  • Direct Recipient Opt-In: When a recipient receives their first SMS notification from Honey Badger, they are asked to reply YES to confirm they consent to receiving messages. Messages will only continue after the recipient confirms their opt-in.
  • Account Registration Opt-In: Users who create a Honey Badger account can opt in to receive SMS messages by providing their phone number and checking the consent checkbox, which states: "I agree to receive SMS text messages from Honey Badger regarding gift notifications, challenge reminders, and account updates. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel at any time."
Consent Language: By opting in to Honey Badger SMS messages, you agree to receive text messages from Honey Badger at the phone number you provide. Consent is not a condition of any purchase. You may revoke consent at any time by replying STOP.

Types of Messages We Send

Honey Badger sends the following types of SMS messages:

  • Gift Notifications: Initial message alerting recipients that they have received a gift and providing details about the associated challenge.
  • Challenge Reminders: Periodic reminders about active challenges (frequency determined by sender settings).
  • Completion Confirmations: Messages confirming when a challenge has been completed and the gift is available.
  • Account Notifications: Important account-related updates for registered users.

Message Frequency

The frequency of messages varies based on gift and challenge settings:

  • Initial Notification: One message when a gift is sent.
  • Reminders: Based on sender configuration (none, daily, or weekly).
  • Completion Message: One message when the challenge is completed.

Typical recipients receive 1-5 messages per gift, depending on challenge duration and reminder settings.

Opting Out

To stop receiving SMS messages from Honey Badger:

Reply STOP to any message you receive from us.

You will receive a confirmation message and will no longer receive SMS messages from our service.

Note: Opting out of SMS messages will not affect your ability to receive gifts. You can still access gifts through email or the Honey Badger website.

Getting Help

Reply HELP to any message for support information.

Supported carriers include (but are not limited to): AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and other major US carriers.

Privacy & Data Protection

Your phone number and personal information are handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy. No mobile information collected as part of the Honey Badger SMS opt-in will be shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

We only use your phone number to deliver gift notifications, challenge reminders, completion confirmations, and account-related messages as described in this policy.

For full details on how we collect, use, and protect your data, please review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
